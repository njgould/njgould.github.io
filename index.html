FlowVue

FlowVue is a cashflow visualisation tool built for integration with Xero.

Cashflow fluctuations are a common scenario in small business, and it's often difficult for business owners to accurately predict future cashflows.

The cashflow reports that are provided within Xero typically don't account for payroll or other expenses, so can often be misleading due to missing data.

Flowvue leverages the AR and AP data in Xero, and synthesises payroll and any other additional cashflow requirements to create a comprehensive picture of future cashflows.

Flowvue assists small business owners in managing cashflow, and provides clear insight around future cashflows. This allows better informed management decisions, and less stress!

  • Live Sync AP and AR transactions from Xero
  • Includes customisable Payroll forecasts based on existing Xero Payruns
  • Incorporates any other upcoming income and commitments
  • All data is synthesised into a live graph for quick and easy reference
  • Individual transaction level detail available for every data point on the graph
  • Multi User access out of the box - Share your flowvue account with your team
  • Setup unlimited organisations with seperate forecasts if required
  • Drill down on transactions, and click through to Xero for quick and easy amendments
  • Complete transaction listing with rolling balance, exportable to csv
